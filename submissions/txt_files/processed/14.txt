    Contribute an Idea: Contribution Overview This submission proposes a web-based annotation layer focused initially on the works of William Shakespeare. For centuries, scholars and readers have annotated Shakespeare’s plays with footnotes and glosses. Yet, most online editions—including high-traffic projects like Open Source Shakespeare—lack accessible footnotes. This effort would establish a collaborative, trusted, and extensible annotation system allowing footnotes and glosses to be layered directly onto public web texts. With a focus on educational value, transparency, and collaborative authorship, this system would begin with Shakespeare but extend to other canonical and public domain texts.
