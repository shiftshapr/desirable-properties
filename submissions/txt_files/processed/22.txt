Contribute an Idea: Title Towards Decentralized Applications: Rethinking Control Power and Data Exchange in Named-Data Networking Contribution Overview This submission presents a comprehensive framework and prototype for decentralized applications built on Named-Data Networking (NDN), aimed at resolving centralization in control power over user data, identity, and communications. Through the design, implementation, and evaluation of "NDN Workspace," the work demonstrates how semantic naming, data-centric security, and distributed data synchronization can enable direct, secure, and serverless peer-to-peer collaboration. The study also contributes critical insights into naming schemes, trust schema enforcement, and the broader ecosystem architecture required to support real-time collaborative applications outside the cloud paradigm.
