**Desirable Properties of the Meta-Layer (DP1–DP20)**

These 21 canonical Desirable Properties (DPs) form the evaluative core of the Meta-Layer Initiative. Submissions are assessed in terms of how they address, support, extend, or clarify these properties.

1. **DP1 – Federated Authentication & Accountability**
   Enable trustable identity across environments without centralization.

2. **DP2 – Participant Agency and Empowerment**
   Prioritize self-sovereignty, consent, and participant-defined interaction boundaries.

3. **DP3 – Adaptive Governance Supporting an Exponentially Growing Community**
   Support layered, adaptable, and context-responsive decision-making mechanisms.

4. **DP4 – Data Sovereignty and Privacy**
   Ensure that data remains owned and governed by its creators or relevant communities.

5. **DP5 – Decentralized Namespace**
   Provide a human-meaningful namespace with decentralized creation and resolution.

6. **DP6 – Commerce**
   Enable economic exchange mechanisms and programmable value transfer.

7. **DP7 – Simplicity and Interoperability**
   Prefer modular, open, and minimal systems that interoperate across contexts.

8. **DP8 – Collaborative Environment and Meta-Communities**
   Foster shared infrastructure and knowledge layers across groups and domains.

9. **DP9 – Developer and Community Incentives**
   Align rewards with public-good contributions and sustainable open-source ecosystems.

10. **DP10 – Education**
    Advance learning systems that support individual and collective epistemic growth.

11. **DP11 – Safe and Ethical AI**
    Ensure AI systems operate transparently, responsibly, and in line with ethical principles.

12. **DP12 – Community-based AI Governance**
    Create frameworks where communities guide how AI operates in their context.

13. **DP13 – AI Containment**
    Develop mechanisms to constrain and audit AI systems within contextual boundaries.

14. **DP14 – Trust and Transparency**
    Promote verifiable processes, open provenance, and legibility of system logic.

15. **DP15 – Security and Provenance**
    Guarantee cryptographic integrity and track the source and transformations of information.

16. **DP16 – Roadmap and Milestones**
    Provide clear trajectories and markers of progress for infrastructure development.

17. **DP17 – Financial Sustainability**
    Support mechanisms for long-term economic viability without exploitative models.

18. **DP18 – Feedback Loops and Reputation**
    Implement mechanisms for evaluation, review, and iterative improvement.

19. **DP19 – Amplifying Presence and Community Engagement**
    Enhance human connection, visibility, and responsiveness within ecosystems.

20. **DP20 – Community Ownership**
    Ensure collective stewardship and co-ownership of shared digital environments.

21. **DP21 - Multi-Modal** Enable systems capable of overlaying and integrating conceptual, spatial, and virtual information across physical, digital, and emerging AGI domains. Support mobile and other devices. 

---

All submissions must address at least one Desirable Property. These properties are interrelated and mutually reinforcing. Clarifications and extensions must refer explicitly to the DPs they modify.
